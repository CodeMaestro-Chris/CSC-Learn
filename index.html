What you should know


Welcome to CSC LEARN, the premier e-learning platform designed exclusively for Rhema University Nigeria Computer Science students. Here to provide a comprehensive and structured learning experience covering all courses from Year 2 to Year 4.

This platform ensures that students have on-demand access to high-quality study materials, lecture notes, and resources tailored to their curriculum.

frequently asked questions

We've got answers

What is CSC LEARN?

CSC LEARN is a free, online learning platform that offers courses in the computer science department. Our mission is to provide students with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their careers.

CSC LEARN is a place where students can learn, share, and grow together. We offer a wide range of courses, from programming to business, and help students build their skills and confidence.

We bridge the gap between classroom learning and digital convenience, empowering students with the tools they need to excel academically. This platform ensures that students have on-demand access to high-quality study materials, lecture notes, and resources tailored to their curriculum.

How do I get started?

Getting started with CSC LEARN is quick and easy — no stress, just results. Whether you're in 200 level or preparing to graduate, here’s how to begin your learning journey:

1. Create an Account

Sign up with your email and matriculation number to get started for free.

2. Verify Your Email

Check your inbox and confirm your email account for eligibility.

3. Explore Available Courses

Browse through organized Computer Science materials from 200 to 400 level.

4. Make a Payment

Choose a plan and make a quick, secure payment to access all premium content.

5. Learn at Your Pace

Start studying anywhere, anytime — with resources tailored just for you.

How it works?

CSC LEARN is designed to make studying easier, smarter, and more accessible for every Computer Science student at Rhema University. Here’s a quick breakdown of how the platform works:

1. Sign Up or Log In

Create an account using your Rhema University email to access your personalized dashboard.

2. Explore the library

Explore a rich library of Computer Science materials organized by academic year — from 200 to 400 level.

3. Unlock Full Access

Make a one-time or subscription-based payment to unlock premium course materials, including lecture notes, past questions and more.

4. Start Learning

Enjoy organized, easy-to-understand content designed to help you study smarter, pass exams, and master your field.

Is CSC LEARN free?

No, Csc Learn offers one-time payment courses to premium users. However, we offer a free trial for new users to test our platform.

Does CSC LEARN have Year One courses?

No, not yet. Csc Learn offers academic help to students in Year two, Year three and Year four.
